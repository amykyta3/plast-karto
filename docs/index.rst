
Plast Karto
===========

This is the home for maps of Пластові Оселі in the US.

To download the latest maps, see your favorite оселя's page in the sidebar.


How can I contribute?
---------------------

Check out the `issues page <https://github.com/amykyta3/plast-karto/issues>`_.
If something is `marked as "Help Wanted" <https://github.com/amykyta3/plast-karto/issues?q=is%3Aopen+is%3Aissue+label%3A%22Help+Wanted%22>`_,
it means I could use your help!

Field Surveys
^^^^^^^^^^^^^
The biggest thing I need help with is collecting survey data. If you have a
handheld GPS, notebook, and love exploring our amazing Пластові оселі, you can help!

Check out any `issues marked as "Survey Needed" <https://github.com/amykyta3/plast-karto/issues?q=is%3Aopen+is%3Aissue+label%3A%22Survey+Needed%22>`_ for
details on what needs to be done.

Here are `some tips on how to do an effective field survey <https://github.com/amykyta3/plast-karto/wiki/How-to-survey-using-GPS>`_.

Report inaccuracies
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
Is a map out of date because there is a new road or building?
Is a trail overgrown and doesn't really exist anymore?
Let me know by creating `an issue ticket <https://github.com/amykyta3/plast-karto/issues>`_
(or just email me at ``amykyta3 at gmail``).
